It could be often confused with migraine, tension headache or other primary headache syndromes. This connection is thought to cause referred pain to the occiput and/or eyes. Neck trauma, whiplash, strain or chronic spasm of the scalp, neck or shoulder muscles can increase the area`s sensitivity similar to the allodynia seen in chronic migraines. A lower pain threshold makes patient more susceptible to more severe pain. For this reason, early diagnosis and therapeutic intervention are very important.&nbsp;</p><p>About 70% of CGH cases involve pathology of C2-3 zygapophyseal joint.&nbsp;</p><p><strong>What is the epidemiology of CGH?</strong></p><p>CGH is a rare condition that appears in people between the age of 30-44 years.
